About

Genesis Geomatics is a multi-disciplined company specializing in Geomatics Engineering and Land Surveying. We have Alberta Land Surveyors, Certified Survey Technologists, Geomatics Engineering Technologists, and CAD technicians on our team, all of whom have years of expertise and dedication.

Dedication

By offering an amazing product and the highest level of value and service to our clients, Genesis Geomatics aims to lead the land surveying sector. Our goal is to accomplish this by offering superior, individualised service, hiring competent, qualified personnel, and delivering reliable data collecting and reporting.
